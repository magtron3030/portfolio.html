About Me

Welcome to the portfolio page of Maggie McDowell! I'm so happy you have come to check out my work and some of my favorite accomplishments. Before coding, I was a business owner doing residential interior painting. This type of work took drive and stamina as well as a creative eye and a love for design. I decided to switch into the coding world when I found out I was pregnant and wanted something I could work from home and still be able to focus on my family. I love the idea of building new applications and putting a creative touch to them.
